One Hundred Sentence

1️⃣ There is considerable debate over how we should react if we detect a signal from an alien civilization.

2️⃣ The kid is walking with a brisk pace.

  • brisk: 活跃的，有活力的，轻快的

3️⃣ His father is a prosperous businessman so he never worries about money.

  • prosperous: 成功的，富裕的

4️⃣ Skin exposed to sunlight for a long time is prone to cancer.

  • prone: 易于，很可能的

5️⃣ The tight schedule had driven him to the brink of a nervous breakdown.

  • brink: 边缘

1️⃣ The lure of fame and money tends to make people commit crimes.

  • lure: 吸引力

2️⃣ Periodic checks are carried out on the equipment.

  • periodic: 周期的，间歇的

3️⃣ The novel that depicts scenes from mythology can cultivate your interest in traditional culture.

  • depict: 描绘(艺术形式)，表现

4️⃣ The patent runs out in three years time.

  • patent : 专利

5️⃣ Leaflets have been delivered to every household.

  • deliver : 投递，发送

1️⃣ A bridge collapsed last week because of the flood.

  • collapse :崩溃，倒闭，失败，瓦解

2️⃣ I think it’s a terrific idea.

  • terrific :极好的，了不起的

3️⃣ So if you want to compliment someone on the work they have done and imitate it, just make sure you do it the right way to avoid committing plagiarism.

  • compliment : 赞美，称赞，问候

4️⃣ Bella was on the verge of tears when she heard the news.

  • verge : 边缘、极限，将近

5️⃣ There was no grief in his eyes, only anger.

  • 悲伤，伤痛

1️⃣ The missing girl is presumed dead.

  • presume :假设，指定

2️⃣ Speed is of the essence in dealing with an emergency.

  • essence : 本质，精髓，实质

3️⃣ The best plan is to take in new information in the morning and then try to consolidate it into memory during the afternoon.

  • consolidate : 巩固，强化

4️⃣ This ideal of independence also contains a tremendous amount of unselfishness.

  • tremendous : 极大地，巨大的，极好的
  • a amount of : 数量

5️⃣ His conspicuous red hair made him easy to be recognized in the crowd.

  • conspicuous :明显的，显著的

Among other things 除此之外

get insight into 深入了解

lie in 位于

refer to 参考，涉及

in an instance
